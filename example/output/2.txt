1. Roof: The roof should be inspected and maintained at least once a year, preferably in the spring or fall.

2. HVAC system: The HVAC system should be serviced and cleaned at least once a year, ideally before the start of the heating season in the fall.

3. Gutters: Gutters should be cleaned and inspected twice a year, typically in the spring and fall, to ensure they are free of debris and functioning properly.
