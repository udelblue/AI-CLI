1. Roof: Regular maintenance and inspections are essential to ensure that the roof remains structurally sound and watertight. Repairs or replacements may be necessary over time to prevent leaks and water damage.

2. HVAC system: It is important to maintain the heating, ventilation, and air conditioning system in a home to ensure proper functioning and energy efficiency. Regular filter changes, cleaning, and professional inspections can help prevent breakdowns and ensure optimal performance.

3. Plumbing: Regular maintenance of the plumbing system is crucial to prevent leaks, clogs, and other issues that can result in water damage and costly repairs. Checking for leaks, insulating pipes in cold weather, and addressing any plumbing concerns promptly can help maintain the integrity of the system.
