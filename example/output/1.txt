1. Roof: Regularly inspect and maintain the roof of a house to ensure there are no leaks, missing shingles, or other damages that could lead to water infiltration.

2. HVAC system: Proper maintenance of heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems is essential for optimal performance and energy efficiency. Change filters regularly, schedule annual inspections, and address any issues promptly.

3. Exterior siding: Maintain the exterior siding of a house by cleaning it regularly, repairing any damaged areas, and repainting or sealing as needed to protect against weather damage and maintain curb appeal.
